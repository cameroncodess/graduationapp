How it works

An idea goes in. A running business comes out.

Every idea moves through the same pipeline — debated, graduated, built, and surfaced to you only when it needs you.

01

The Crucible

A Researcher scopes the market, a Pitch makes the case, and a Critic tears in — until the Critic is genuinely convinced or the idea is killed.

02

Graduated

Ideas that survive the debate land in your portfolio, each rated and tagged by how hands-off it can run.

03

The Workroom

A Planner and Executor build the winner — real copy, pages, drafts, deliverables — checking each other's work.

04

Master Inbox

Only the handful of things that truly need you — approve, pay, or send — arrive as one-tap decisions with a recommendation.

The team

A studio of agents, each with one job.

They run on your Claude subscription and hand off to each other automatically — from first idea to a portfolio that manages itself.

Seeds the ideas

Framer

Proposes fresh, specific, non-derivative business ideas across markets.

Scopes the market

Researcher

Finds who wants it, real proof of demand, and what competitors already do.

Makes the case

Pitch

The optimistic founder — who pays, the wedge, and why it becomes a company.

Tears in

Critic

The tough investor. Only genuinely convincing ideas graduate — and it rates each one.

Maps the build

Planner

Breaks a graduated idea into concrete steps and splits agent-work from what needs you.

Builds it

Executor

Does the real work — research, copy, drafts, deliverables — live on the web.

Watches the portfolio

Warden

Ranks every idea, restarts stalled winners, and concentrates effort on what's working.

Preps your inbox

Scribe

Pre-writes the step-by-step and ready-to-send drafts before you ever open a task.
